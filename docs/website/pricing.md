## Frequently Asked Questions

### What counts as a "client"?
A client is any individual or business entity that HeshbonAI actively messages for documents. Inactive clients (no outreach in a billing cycle) are not counted. You only pay for clients HeshbonAI is working on.

### How does the 14-day free trial work?
Sign up, connect your WhatsApp Business account, upload your client list, and HeshbonAI starts collecting documents immediately. Full access to all Growth plan features. No credit card required to start. If you don't convert, your data is deleted within 30 days.

### Can I change plans later?
Yes. Upgrade or downgrade anytime. Changes take effect on your next billing cycle. If you grow past 50 clients on Starter, you'll automatically get the Growth rate ($10/client) -- your per-client cost actually goes down.

### Is there an annual discount?
Yes. Pay annually and get **2 months free** (effectively 17% off). Annual pricing: Starter $150/client/year. Growth $100/client/year.

### What if a client doesn't use WhatsApp?
HeshbonAI can fall back to SMS for clients without WhatsApp (available on Growth and Enterprise plans). For the Israeli market, WhatsApp penetration is 80%+, so this covers the vast majority of clients.

### Do my clients need to install anything?
No. Zero downloads. Zero signups. HeshbonAI messages them on WhatsApp -- the app they already have open. They just reply with a photo or file. That's it.

### How does HeshbonAI handle sensitive financial documents?
All documents are encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256). HeshbonAI operates through the official WhatsApp Business API -- the same infrastructure used by banks and healthcare providers. We do not store document contents after delivery to your dashboard. SOC 2 Type II certification in progress.

### Can I customize what HeshbonAI says to my clients?
Yes. Growth and Enterprise plans include custom message templates. You control the tone, the firm name, the specific language. Most firms use our optimized defaults (82% response rate) and tweak from there.

### What accounting software does HeshbonAI integrate with?
Growth plan: CSV and API export compatible with any system. Enterprise plan: Direct integrations with Hashavshevet, Priority, QuickBooks, Xero, and SAP. Custom integrations available on request.

### What's your cancellation policy?
Cancel anytime. No penalties. No lock-in. Monthly billing stops at the end of your current cycle. We'll export your data on request.
