## FAQ

### Section Header
# Frequently Asked Questions

**How does HeshbonAI message my clients?**
HeshbonAI uses the official WhatsApp Business API. Messages come from your firm's branded WhatsApp Business number with your firm name and logo. To your clients, it looks like a message from a team member at your firm.

**Will my clients know it's automated?**
The assistant uses a human name you choose (e.g., "Maya") and writes in a professional, natural tone. Most clients cannot tell it is automated. Two firms in our pilot had clients ask if they hired a new assistant.

**What happens if a client asks a question the assistant can't answer?**
HeshbonAI responds with "Let me check with [Firm Name] and get back to you" and immediately flags it for your team. It never guesses or makes up answers.

**Is it available in Hebrew?**
Yes. HeshbonAI is Hebrew-first, built for the Israeli market. English, Arabic, and Russian are also supported.

**How long does setup take?**
Most firms are up and running in 15 minutes. Upload your client list, configure your document types, and HeshbonAI handles the rest.

**Can I control when messages are sent?**
Yes. You set the schedule. HeshbonAI never messages before 08:00 or after 20:00 by default, and respects Shabbat and holidays. You can customize timing per client.

**What if I want to stop a conversation?**
You can pause or take over any client conversation at any time from the dashboard. You are always in control.

**How is my data protected?**
All documents are encrypted in transit and at rest. Your firm's data is isolated -- never shared with other firms. We use Meta-approved WhatsApp Business API infrastructure and are pursuing SOC 2 Type II certification.
