# HeshbonAI -- Stop Chasing Clients for Documents

## The Problem

Your firm spends 10-15 hours every week calling, emailing, and WhatsApping clients for documents they should have sent last month. Follow-ups fall through the cracks. Deadlines get missed. Tax season turns into 60-hour weeks of chasing, not accounting.

## The Solution

HeshbonAI is an AI agent that contacts your clients on WhatsApp, requests missing documents, follows up automatically, and collects everything into one dashboard. You tell HeshbonAI what you need. HeshbonAI handles the chasing.

## How It Works

**Step 1: Upload your client list** -- CSV or manual entry. Name, phone number, done.

**Step 2: Create a request** -- Select clients, pick document types (invoices, bank statements, receipts, etc.), set a deadline.

**Step 3: HeshbonAI takes over** -- Sends WhatsApp messages, follows up on Day 3/5/7, confirms receipt, stores documents, and flags anything overdue. You watch from the dashboard.
