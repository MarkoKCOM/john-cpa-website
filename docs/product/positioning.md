# HeshbonAI -- Product Positioning

## One-Liner

HeshbonAI is the AI assistant for CPA firms -- it automates client follow-up and document collection through WhatsApp.

## Category

AI workflow assistant for accounting firms.

## Core Promise

Automate the admin work around client document collection without changing how clients already communicate.

## Elevator Pitch (30 seconds)

Every CPA firm wastes 10-15 hours per week calling and emailing clients for documents they never send on time. HeshbonAI replaces that entirely. You assign the documents you need from each client, and HeshbonAI handles the rest -- sending WhatsApp messages, following up automatically, confirming receipt, and tracking everything in an operational dashboard. Your clients respond on WhatsApp because that is where they already communicate. Your team gets full visibility into what is in, what is missing, and who needs attention.

## Positioning Statement

For Israeli CPA firms that lose hours every week chasing clients for documents, HeshbonAI is an AI-powered WhatsApp assistant that automates follow-up, document collection, status tracking, and operational visibility. Unlike email reminders or client portals that nobody checks, HeshbonAI meets clients on WhatsApp -- where Israeli open rates exceed 90% -- and handles the entire back-and-forth conversation without CPA involvement.

---

## Product Scope

HeshbonAI covers four core capabilities:

1. **Document collection** -- request and receive documents from clients via WhatsApp
2. **Follow-up automation** -- smart, scheduled follow-ups that escalate based on deadlines
3. **Status tracking** -- per-client, per-document visibility into what is in and what is missing
4. **Operational visibility** -- firm-wide dashboard showing team workload, deadline risk, and collection progress

## Key Differentiators

### 1. WhatsApp-Native
HeshbonAI works where clients already are -- WhatsApp. No app downloads, no portal logins, no new accounts. Clients receive a message, snap a photo, and send it back. Open rates exceed 90%.

### 2. Firm-Branded Assistant
HeshbonAI does not show up as a generic AI. It operates as a named assistant from the firm (e.g., "Maya from Cohen & Co."). Clients trust it because it feels like a real team member.

### 3. Hebrew-First
Built for the Israeli market from day one. Hebrew is the default language, not a translation layer on top of English. Cultural context (Shabbat, local holidays, communication norms) is built in.

### 4. Operational Dashboard
Not just document collection -- HeshbonAI gives firm owners visibility they have never had. Who is behind? Which clients are at risk of missing deadlines? How is the team performing? All in one view.

### vs. Doing Nothing (Current State -- Manual Chasing)
- HeshbonAI saves 10-15 hours/week of staff time
- Zero documents lost in email or WhatsApp threads
- Automatic escalation means nothing falls through the cracks
- Audit trail of every request and response

### vs. Liscio (US-Based Client Portal)
- Liscio requires clients to download an app and create an account. Adoption rate: 30-50%
- HeshbonAI uses WhatsApp -- clients already have it, no download, no signup
- Liscio is built for the US market (English only, US tax workflows)
- Liscio costs $50-100/user/month. HeshbonAI prices per firm, not per user
- Liscio is a portal clients must visit. HeshbonAI comes to the client

### vs. ClientWindow (UK Document Collection)
- ClientWindow is UK-only, not available in Israel
- ClientWindow is a "dumb pipe" -- sends requests but does not follow up autonomously
- No AI. No smart escalation. No deadline tracking
- No WhatsApp integration (email and SMS only)

### vs. FinBot (Israeli OCR/Bookkeeping Bot)
- FinBot focuses on OCR and data entry, not document collection
- FinBot does not chase clients -- it processes what is already received
- No autonomous follow-up capability
- Complementary product, not a competitor (HeshbonAI collects, FinBot processes)

### vs. Generic WhatsApp Broadcast Tools
- Broadcast tools send messages. HeshbonAI holds conversations
- No document tracking, no follow-up logic, no dashboard
- Cannot handle replies or confirm receipt
- Not purpose-built for CPA workflows

## Objection Handling

### "Is client data secure?"

HeshbonAI is built for financial document handling from day one. All documents are encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256). We use the official WhatsApp Business API through Meta-approved providers -- the same infrastructure banks and insurance companies use. Documents are stored in your firm's isolated environment, never shared across firms. We are pursuing SOC 2 Type II certification and will comply with Israeli Privacy Protection Law (PPPA) requirements.

### "Will my clients feel comfortable getting messages from an assistant?"

Your clients already communicate via WhatsApp daily. Messages come from your firm's branded WhatsApp Business number with your firm name and logo. The assistant uses your firm's name and a human-sounding name you choose. Clients typically cannot tell it is automated. In our testing, 85% of clients preferred WhatsApp requests over email or phone calls. Clients can reply naturally ("here is the invoice" + photo) and the assistant handles it.

### "What if it sends the wrong thing or makes a mistake?"

HeshbonAI only sends messages based on the documents you assign and the schedules you configure. It does not improvise document requests or make up deadlines. Every message follows templates you approve, personalized with the client's name and specific documents needed. The dashboard shows you exactly what was sent to whom and when. You can pause or override any conversation at any time. If a client asks a question the assistant cannot answer, it flags it for you immediately instead of guessing.

### "My clients are not tech-savvy."

That is exactly why HeshbonAI works. Your clients do not need to download anything, create an account, or learn a new system. They get a WhatsApp message, take a photo of the document, and send it back. If your clients can send a photo on WhatsApp -- and they can -- they can use HeshbonAI.

### "I already have a system that works."

If you are spending less than 2 hours per week chasing documents and your on-time submission rate is above 90%, your system works and you do not need HeshbonAI. For everyone else: the average CPA firm spends 10-15 hours/week on document collection. At a billing rate of 300 NIS/hour, that is 3,000-4,500 NIS/week in lost productivity. HeshbonAI costs a fraction of one week's lost time.

### "What about Hebrew?"

Hebrew is the default language. HeshbonAI is built for the Israeli market first. English, Arabic, and Russian are also supported for firms with diverse client bases.
